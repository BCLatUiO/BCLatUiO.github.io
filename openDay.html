Blockchain Lab Open Day

Blockchain Lab Open Day

The Blockchain Lab at UiO is organizing an open day and inviting participation of organizations and enterprises from the public and private sectors in Norway that are interested in the exploitation of blockchain technologies. The goals of the open day are:

  • To share the vision and mission of the Lab with prominent representatives of the public and private sectors in Norway,
  • To present our research and innovation ambitions as well as the areas strategic for the Lab,
  • To organize breakout sessions on topics of potential interest to the participants. The goal of the breakout sessions is to establish strategic areas having a critical mass, wherein the partners will have complementary expertise.

By the end of the open day, we hope to identify future Lab partners and consortium members for topical Lab initiatives. We further hope to collect feedback regarding specific collaboration and catalyzation mechanisms within the Lab.

The agenda of the open day will consist of (a) talks by the Lab Steering Committee members and key industrial players and (b) aforementioned breakout sessions. Breakout sessions will be organised around three topics based on your feedback. These may include for example:

  • Assessing, configuring and benchmarking blockchain technologies for your use case
  • Legal/Regulatory frameworks in relation to blockchain, from data protection to fintech
  • Blockchain use in energy/SmartCities/IoT
  • Public sector smart services (such as digital infrastructure, open data ecosystems, and self-sovereign identity)
  • Digital assets beyond Bitcoin (such as Decentralised Finance, Central Bank Digital Currencies, Non-Fungible Tokens)
  • Blockchain-enabled business models and SDG impact

Please note that our focus in the discussions is not on how to build a use case (design and solutions) and not on sharing technical details behind those solutions. Instead, our focus is on finding complementary expertise for the purpose of partnership, on the concrete ways to utilize blockchain technologies in different contexts, and on the catalyst mechanisms the Blockchain Lab will be using.

Please register (link) if you plan to attend the open day. While the talks will be open to the public, participation in the breakout sessions will only be possible for registered and pre-approved participants! If multiple people from the same organization attend, a separate registration is required for each.

You are welcome to contact us at bclab-openday@ifi.uio.no, if you have any questions or inquiries about the open day.

The date is being finalized but it will be between 29th of Nov and 3rd of Dec.
