Contact Us

We welcome inquiries in following cases:

  • From media outlets requiring technical expertise for popular articles
  • From public sector or industry seeking technical competence in blockchain solutions
  • From academic institutions or industry leading a consortium and seeking partners with complementary expertise
  • From public sector or industry interested in hiring graduates for permanent blockchain-related jobs. Summer internships may also be of interest but only at large enterprises.


Inquiries about partnership in the Lab are welcome

  • From public sector or large enterprises that focus on use cases
  • From large enterprises and organisations that are technology providers

An inquiry about partnership needs to specify the resources the partner is willing to commit. For providers of a popular blockchain technology, it could be access to the technology along with competence. For providers of an important use case, it can be, e.g., the design document specifying the requirements or access to the data (properly handled in accordance to the regulations).





Do not hesitate to contact us in any of the cases mentioned above!
