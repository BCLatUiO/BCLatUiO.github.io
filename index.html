Why Blockchain?

Blockchain is a groundbreaking paradigm for secure, accountable, and transparent storage, which goes well beyond cryptocurrencies. We are witnessing how the blockchain transforms digital infrastructures and data exchange platforms in a variety of domains, with broad applications to to digital trust, digital identities, cybersecurity, data provenance, and data governance as well as smart contracts (see our research page for additional information).

We put particular emphasis on “green” blockchain that does not consume exorbitant amounts of energy in contrast to Bitcoin. Green blockchain is entirely viable as many permissionless systems use lightweight proof-of-work or alternative proof-of-X methods while permissioned blockchain do not have any need in proof-of-X.

Blockchain is of strategic significance at the European arena, as corroborated by the establishment of the European Blockchain Services Infrastructure (EBSI). Governments in North America, Europe, and Asia are advancing blockchain-related strategy and legislation, reflecting their significant interest towards the advent and utilization of the technology.
